La Petit Francais

La Petit Francais was founded by Laura Mitchell, a sweets devotee with a desire to create beautiful desserts that taste as good as they look. Luxury Cake Company can create everything from 2D cakes, 3D cakes, children's cakes, wedding cakes, cupcakes, custom cookies, to baked donuts, various mini desserts and custom dessert table design. The menu highlights traditional American flavors created by using high quality ingredients.
